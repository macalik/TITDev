Why do we use SSO and is it safe?

First, read this: http://community.eveonline.com/news/dev-blogs/eve-online-sso-and-what-you-need-to-know/

It's a little old since it says something about a "SSO Limited Trial", but it is now open to all developers.

TL;DR: We NEVER see your username or password.

You can also stop giving us your information whenever you want, just like your api keys.

Similar link used for SSO: https://community.eveonline.com/support/third-party-applications/

What does this mean for our corp?

  • We don't have to save your username or password. No worries about getting your creditials stolen/hacked from our databases.
  • We can confirm your identity. If you ever change your corp/alliance, you will automatically have no access to our services.
  • It's safer. We don't have to ever worry about encrypting your connection. We won't have to pass around log-in credentials.
